We record some notable cases of AI contributions to Erdős problems.

We hope that it is useful to understand AI trajectories and to gather preliminary information for further investigation.

## Problem [[1026]](https://www.erdosproblems.com/1026)

*Timeframe*. Dec 2025.

*Significance*. Showcased various forms of AI assistance and usefulness in a mathematical collaboration.

*Final outcome*. Solution with strengthened conclusion.

*Resources*. This [blog post](https://terrytao.wordpress.com/2025/12/08/the-story-of-erdos-problem-126/).

## Problem [[728]](https://www.erdosproblems.com/728)

*Timeframe*. Jan 2026.

*Significance*. Regarded as the first autonomous and nontrivial solution to an Erdős problem by AI systems.

*Final outcome*. General result giving solutions to this problem, [[729]](https://www.erdosproblems.com/729), and [[401]](https://www.erdosproblems.com/401).

*Resources*. This [arXiv preprint](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.07421). This [blog post](https://www.erdosproblems.com/forum/thread/blog:2).

## Problem [[1196]](https://www.erdosproblems.com/1196)

*Timeframe*. Apr 2026.

*Significance*. Obtained solution to a problem that human experts have already spent substantial effort on.

*Final outcome*. Novel technique resolving this problem, [[1217]](https://www.erdosproblems.com/1217), [[164]](https://www.erdosproblems.com/164), and two other conjectures. Inspired solution to [[858]](https://www.erdosproblems.com/858).

*Resources*. This [arXiv preprint](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.00301). This [blog post](https://terrytao.wordpress.com/2026/05/03/primitive-sets-and-von-mangoldt-chains-erdos-problem-1196-and-beyond/).

## Problem [[90]](https://www.erdosproblems.com/90)

*Timeframe*. May 2026.

*Significance*. Resolved major open problem in a mathematical area with prior widespread expert attention.

*Final outcome*. Also disproved [[92]](https://www.erdosproblems.com/92). Admissible exponent recorded as a new [optimization constant](https://teorth.github.io/optimizationproblems/constants/84a.html). Inspired this [arXiv preprint](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.28781) on [[52]](https://www.erdosproblems.com/52).

*Resources*. This [arXiv preprint](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.20695). This [arXiv preprint](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.20579). This [MathOverflow question](https://mathoverflow.net/questions/511514/what-is-the-unit-distance-exponent). This [blog post](https://www.erdosproblems.com/forum/thread/blog:6).
